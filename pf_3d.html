3D Materials (UDK)

These are materials that I have created rendered in the Unreal Engine 3.

I created every diffuse texture sample digitally by hand with additional layers from algorithmic generators (such as noise).

Specular maps were manually created from diffuse layers. Then normal maps were generated using SSbump Generator 5.3.

Some materials also include parallax maps for added detail of cavities and bumps.

Materials are previewed below with embedded Gfycat videos of 720x480 video originally rendered at 1920x1080 uncompressed.
